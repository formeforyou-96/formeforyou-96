Plastic Recycling Crisis: What You Should Know

1. Chemicals Concern Overhaul Urgently Needed

Source: Reuters
Only 9% of plastic ever produced has been recycled; systemic reforms are needed.

2. Recycling Plastic is Practically Impossible

Source: NPR
Only about 5% of US plastic waste is recycled. Recycling alone is not a solution.

3. Plastic Producers Misled Consumers

Source: The Guardian
Producers knew recycling was not sustainable but still promoted it to consumers.

5 Easy Ways to Reduce Plastic Use

  • Use reusable shopping bags.
  • Carry a tumbler or personal cup.
  • Choose products with minimal packaging.
  • Use reusable utensils and straws.
  • Recycle properly by cleaning plastics.
